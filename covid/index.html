Coronavirus Anxiety Index

Let's be real. The novel coronavirus originating in Wuhan, China (COVID-19) is pretty scary, regardless of what the future holds. That has real impacts on local society. Surgical masks have become the go-to symbol of pandemic fear, the first visible preventative action people often take. So I started tracking how I see it during my commute. Feel free to join me.
It'll remain anonymous and, for the moment, completely unscientific. Just an experiment with turning what we see into data. Stay safe.

How did we calculate these two numbers?
Total uses of force includes any time an officer used force during an arrest and filled out a use-of-force form, as required. Because multiple officers might use force during a single arrest, or incident, the rate of force was calculated using the overall number of incidents.
